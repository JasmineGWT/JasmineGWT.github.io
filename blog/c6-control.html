Triggers when programming

I think for me one of my favorite parts of programming are the "Shit, I have no idea what I'm doing" moments because when you figure it out the feeling is awesome. So although I hate getting stuck I also love getting unstuck. Along with that though, a big thing I find difficult is when I have done a task and its finished and works but its not amazing. Like when I know I can improve on it but I also know that it makes no difference in terms of how the user would interact with it and it makes me not efficient since I'm spending so much time perfecting things when I could be doing other tasks. That really kills me a little bit inside.
