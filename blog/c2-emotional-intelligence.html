What learnings did you take from the chapter?

From this I learnt just how much research has gone into emotional intelligence and wellbeing. I knew it was a somewhat well known concept but I didn't realize the huge amount of studies supporting the use of it. What I also found really interesting is that a lot of the time the way it's explained is too abstract or too many long doctor words are used therefore making it somewhat hard to understand, but I really enjoyed the way everything was explained here as it's easy to understand and comprehend.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I've done this many times before and contrary to what the book says, I always find it really difficult. My mind tends to immediatly wonder off to random things that happened 5 years ago, what's for dinner, what am I doing tomorrow, what's the meaning of life... All of that type of stuff. So I just need to keep practicing being in the moment and focusing on nothing but what is around me.
