What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

By the time I'd finsihed reading the chapter and got a chance to actually have the conversation there was no one around to actually try this with. So I read everyone elses blog to see how they found it and it was interesting to see all the different perspectives. I will try my best to do it this week since it does sound so interesting.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

To be honest this week I didn't really have time to be mindful but in saying that I did notice that I hadn't left the house in a few days (other than going to work on Monday) so I made sure that over the weekend I went out and saw friends, went to the beach, called my dad etc. Just making sure to keep my mental health up by being connected to others and having a good time. Sometimes this helps a lot more than being mindfull. However, I will 100% continue to practice mindfulness as I know it is a really important/helpful skill.
